Astronomy

19th-century Sydney Observatory, Australia (1873) [3] 19th-century Quito Astronomical Observatory is located 12 minutes south of the Equator in Quito, Ecuador.[4] Astronomy (from the Greek ἀστρονομία from ἄστρον astron, "star" and -νομία -nomia from νόμος nomos, "law" or "culture") means "law of the stars" (or "culture of the stars" depending on the translation). Astronomy should not be confused with astrology, the belief system which claims that human affairs are correlated with the positions of celestial objects.[5] Although the two fields share a common origin, they are now entirely distinct.[6]
